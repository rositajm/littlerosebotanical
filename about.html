Welcome to Little Rose Botanical

Little Rose Botanical is a space where plant enthusiasts and curious minds come together to learn, grow, and explore the fascinating world of plants. Whether you’re looking for tips on plant care, insights into plant evolution, or DIY projects to bring your green thumb to life, you’ll find it here.

About Me

I’m Rosie, a botanist with a passion for sharing the wonders of the plant world. My journey into botany started with a simple curiosity about how plants grow and evolved into a lifelong commitment to understanding and teaching others about them. From working in nurseries to conducting experiments, I’ve spent years cultivating my knowledge of plants and their care.

My Mission

Little Rose Botanical is a place for learning, discovery, and connection. My goal is to share my knowledge of plants in a way that’s accessible, practical, and engaging. From diagnosing plant problems to experimenting with new ways to encourage growth, I aim to help you become more confident and curious in your plant journey.
